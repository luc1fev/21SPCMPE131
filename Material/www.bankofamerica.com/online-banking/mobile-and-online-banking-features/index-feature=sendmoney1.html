The Bank of America Mobile Banking app

together with Zelle

lets you securely send or request money to or from almost anyone

no matter where they bank.

To get started, open your app, sign in, and select Transfer|Send.

If this is your first time sending money, select Begin Setup to accept the agreement.

Next, select Add/Edit your Email/Mobile # to add your contact information

and select the account where you want to receive your money.

When prompted, enter the code to confirm we have your contact information correct.

Now you are set to send and receive money using Zelle in the Bank of America app.

Now, say you want to send $20 to Tim Jones for yesterday's lunch.

In Transfer|Send, tap Send.

Select Tim from your recipients list or + Add from contacts.

Enter the amount, select the account, add an optional note, and tap Send money.

The money, along with a message, is on its way to Tim.

Tim will then get a text or email saying you sent him money

If he is already registered with Zelle, the funds will go right into his bank account.

Otherwise, he'll be guided through an easy registration.

You can also request money, like $15 from Robin for the cab you shared last night.

From Transfer|Send select Request

find Robin in your contacts

enter the amount

and she will instantly be sent a message that you requested the money.

If she's not already using Zelle, she will receive a message with instructions on how to get started.

You can even split a request among your friends, like for the group dinner you paid for last night

Select friends in your contacts.

Enter the total amount of the dinner, $120, and the math will be done for you.

Add an optional note, adjust individual amounts as needed, and the request will be sent instantly.

So, next time you need to pay someone back, just open your Bank of America app.

You can confidently send or request money to or from friends and family

no matter where they bank

and know you're protected by our Mobile Banking Security Guarantee.

Now, it's easy to move money in minutes.

    We strive to provide you with information about products and services you might find interesting and useful. Relationship-based ads and online behavioral advertising help us do that.

This information may be used to deliver advertising on our Sites and offline (for example, by phone, email and direct mail) that's customized to meet specific interests you may have.

    Also, if you opt out of online behavioral advertising, you may still see ads when you sign in to your account, for example through Online Banking or MyMerrill. These ads are based on your specific account relationships with us.
